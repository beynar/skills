# Grammar and Mechanics

For anyone who writes for the admin.

## Capitalization

### Use Sentence Case For:
- Headings
- Subheadings
- Buttons
- Card titles
- Email subject lines

### Do
- Create purchase order
- Manage your store

### Don't
- Create Purchase Order
- Manage Your Store

### Capitalize:
- Proper or trademarked nouns (names of products/brands, countries, people)
- Product names unique to Shopify
- Job titles when they come before or after a person's name

### Do
- Shopify Payments
- Jane Smith, Content Strategist
- The United States

### Don't
- shopify balance
- Jane Smith, content strategist
- the US (unless as adjective)

### Use Lowercase For:
- Features or product terms not unique to Shopify
- Job titles without a name associated with them
- First letter following a /

### Do
- blogs, navigation, admin, page
- The content strategist designed this
- ZIP/postal code

### Don't
- Blogs, Navigation, Admin, Page
- The Content Strategist designed this
- ZIP/Postal code

## Contractions

Use contractions—they're short and conversational.

### Do
- Can't, it's, you're, doesn't

### Don't
- Can not, it is, you are, does not

## Bold

When in doubt, don't bold. Use bold sparingly and only where strong emphasis is required. Don't use bold to create a heading or emphasize proper nouns, merchant input, or checkbox titles.

### Do
- After your first sale, PayPal will email you at **merchant@email.com** with instructions.

### Don't
- Are you sure you want to delete **Sunset T-shirt**?

## Headings

Headings and subheadings should be:
- **Informative and descriptive:** Highlight the most important concept and help merchants understand what they'll find below
- **Concise and scannable:** Use simple, clear language, keep to a single sentence, avoid punctuation, use sentence case

### Do
- Online store dashboard
- Custom reports
- Secure your account with two-step authentication

### Don't
- This is your online store dashboard
- Custom Reports
- Two-step authentication

## Links

Link text should clearly describe where merchants will go or what action occurs.

### Do
- Set up your store with a step-by-step guide
- Show up where shoppers scroll, search, and shop with multichannel integration
- Track customer behavior on your online store to improve your marketing. Learn more

### Don't
- Want to learn more about how to set up your online store? Click here.
- Learn more.

## Lists

Introduce bulleted lists with a colon or a heading.

### Punctuation Rules
- Don't use commas at the end of list items (bulleted or numbered)
- If any list item contains 2+ sentences, punctuate all list items
- If all list items are one sentence or fragments, don't punctuate
- List items always start with a capital letter

### Do
- Buy the domain directly through your Shopify store.
- Buy the domain through a third-party provider and then point it to your Shopify store.
- Transfer the domain from another Shopify store to your Shopify store.

### Don't
- You can add a custom domain to your store in the following ways:
  - Buying the domain from Shopify
  - Transferring

### Actions in a Menu

Actions follow a {verb}+{noun} pattern. If there's enough context, only a verb might be required.

### Do
- Rename, Edit, HTML, Duplicate

### Don't
- HTML, editing options, File name changes

## Numbers, Dates, and Currency

### Numbers
- Use numerals (5 not "five")
- Use commas for numbers with four or more digits (12,000 not "12 k")
- For phone numbers: 1-514-555-1234 (not 613.555.1234)
- For ranges: use en dash without spaces (5–10 products)
- For undefined upward ranges: "$50.00 and up" or "100 or more"
- For downward ranges: "$0.00–$49.99"

### Do
- You have 5 orders to fulfill.
- 12,000
- 5–10 products

### Don't
- You have five orders to fulfill.
- 12 k
- 5 – 10 products

### Dates
- Use the month's full name or 3-letter abbreviation
- Don't use numerals only (not 12/11/24)
- Don't use ordinal indicators (not "January 23rd")

### Do
- December 11, 2024
- Jan 11, 2024
- January 23–April 1

### Don't
- December 11, '24
- 12/11/24
- January 23rd–April 1st

### Time
- Use 12-hour clock followed by am or pm
- Include a space after the last number ("Your package will arrive at 12:35 pm.")
- For time ranges: use en dash with am/pm after both times (3:00 pm–4:00 pm)
- Separate date and time with "at" instead of a comma

### Currency
Currency comes after the dollar amount.

### Do
- $10,000 USD

### Don't
- USD$10,000

### Units of Measurement
- Include a space between number and unit (3.4 lb not 3.4lb)
- Don't pluralize units (4 lb not 4 lbs)
- For multiple measurements, put unit at the end (11.875 × 3.375 × 13.625 cm)
- Use decimals instead of fractions (2.375 lb not 2-3/8 lb)

### Do
- 3.4 lb, 2 kg, 1 cm

### Don't
- 3.4lb, 2kg, 1.875 cm × 3.375 cm × 13.625 cm

## Pronouns

### Addressing Merchants
Always refer to merchants as "you." Don't speak for merchants with "I" or "my."

### Do
- Change your email address in your Profile.

### Don't
- Change your email address in My Profile.

Exception: For getting consent or granting permissions, use "I."

### Do
- I agree to the terms and conditions

### Don't
- You agree the terms and conditions

### Referring to Shopify
Always refer to Shopify as "we," but avoid inserting Shopify into the content as much as possible.

### Do
- Pinterest Buyable Pins will be available in the coming weeks.

### Don't
- We're making Pinterest Buyable Pins available in the coming weeks.

## Punctuation

### Ampersands
Don't use ampersands (&). Spell out "and."

### Do
- Zones and rates

### Don't
- Zones & rates

### Apostrophes
- Use for omitted letters (don't, can't, won't)
- Use for verb contractions (it's, you're, we're)
- Use for possessives: singular nouns add 's (merchant's), plural without s add 's (women's), plural with s add apostrophe (customers')
- Always use curly quotes, not straight quotes

### Colons
Avoid colons in sentences. Introduce bulleted lists with a colon. Don't capitalize the first word after a colon unless it's a proper noun.

### Do
- Correct the following payment information to continue:
- Your store accepts payments with GoCoin and Shopify Payments.

### Don't
- Your store accepts payments with: GoCoin and Shopify Payments.

### Ellipses
Use the ellipsis character (…, not three periods). Use for truncation in the center of a title bar. Avoid for placeholder copy or trailing off sentences.

### Do
- Search files

### Don't
- Start typing to search for files…

### En-Dashes and Em-Dashes
- En dash (–) for fixed ranges without spaces (2006–2013)
- Em dash (—) only if you can't make your message clearer by splitting it into two sentences

### Do
- 2006–2013
- Choose your theme's colors, typography, and pictures—all in one place.

### Don't
- 2006 – 2013
- Choose your theme's design—colors, typography, and pictures—all in one place.

### Exclamation Marks
Avoid them. Only use for really exciting things. Limit yourself to one exclamation mark per page.

### Do
- You've launched your first online store!

### Don't
- You've updated your product title!

### Hyphens
Use to form compound modifiers (two words that combine to modify the noun that follows).

### Do
- Start your free, no-risk, 14-day trial.
- Add weight-based shipping rates.

### Don't
- Start your free, no risk, 14 day trial.
- Add weight based shipping rates.

Join prefixes and suffixes only if there are two vowels beside each other. Never use hyphens in "ecommerce" and "email."

### Periods
- Don't use periods in interface copy
- If text includes 2+ sentences, use periods
- Use periods for list items with 2+ sentences

### Do
- Add products to collections so they're easy to find

### Don't
- Add products to collections so they're easy to find.

### Question Marks
Avoid question marks. Reword into affirmative statements. It's okay to use question marks if you don't know the result:

### Do
- Forgot?
- Did you forget your password?

### Don't
- Reset password?
- Show quantity box?

### Quotation Marks
Always use smart (curly) quotes, not vertical (straight) quotes.

### Semicolons
Avoid if possible. Connect two closely related independent clauses only.

### Do
- My friends made me banana pancakes on my birthday; I've never been happier.

### Don't
- The unicorn was hungry; the grass was brown.

## Spelling

Use American spelling for all external-facing Shopify content. Check Merriam-Webster when in doubt.

### Do
- Color, Center, Canceled

### Don't
- Colour, Centre, Cancelled

## Addresses

Layout address forms in this format:
```
First name                Last name
Address
Apartment, suite, etc. (optional)
City
Country
Province
Postal code
```

Use locale-specific alternatives for province and postal code (e.g., State and ZIP code for the US).

### Do
- Use country's proper name as a noun: "The United States"
- Use abbreviated form for country as adjective: "US currency"

### Don't
- Your store must be located in the US.
- U.S. currency
